Beautiful Signals

A Pōneke event series centered on appropriate digital tech.

Dec 04, 2024

Beautiful Signals is an event series engaging art practices and a series of self-run workshops that explore emerging issues with soil, food, data, democracy and digital sovereignty

This was a project brought together by an emerging group of Pōneke activists/ programmers/ artists/ community organisers. I helped pull the website together, and also hosted a workshop on Māori Data Sovereignty.
