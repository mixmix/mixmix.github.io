May 03, 2017

About

Protozoa is a Cooperative Company registered in New Zealand. It was established by 3 friends as a way to work flexibly with great people, on important problems.

Over the course of it’s existence Protozoa has run in a bunch of ways to support these goals:

  • full income sharing
  • peer mentoring
  • supporting immigration from hostile countries (USA)
  • accessing mortgages by providing more fiscal legibility to banks easily

I was a founding member of the Cooperative. Over the years many different people have been contributors or owners we’ve grown to 5 co-owners.

Pro Bono work

Through collective power we’ve also been able to resource some contributions to community that I’m really proud of. Including but not limited to:

  • Conference Organising
    • Dweb Camp
    • ScuttleCamp
    • Decent Social
  • Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion
    • grant fundraising
    • design + execution of scholarships
    • Tauiwi Tautoko
  • Community Grant Management
    • SSBC Dfinity Grants
    • Scuttlebutt Maintenance
    • Scuttlebutt Grantwriting
